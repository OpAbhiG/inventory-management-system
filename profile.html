About PBMA's

Founded on 2nd October, 1952 by Late Mr. Tukaram Sahadeo Bamankar and fellow blind persons, PBMA started with a small room providing welfare services for the blind community. With support from dedicated individuals like Late Mr. N. M. Merchant and Late Mr. Niranjan Pandya, PBMA has grown into one of India's most recognized organizations for the visually impaired.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive eye care services and eliminate preventable blindness through quality treatment, education, and rehabilitation.

Our Vision

A world where no one suffers from preventable blindness and everyone has access to quality eye care regardless of economic status.
